About Luke’s Guide Services

Raised on the waters of Southeast Texas, Luke turned childhood passion into a profession—guiding families, friends, and first-timers to unforgettable days on the lake.

Luke grew up chasing bass on the quiet farm ponds and sprawling reservoirs around Cleveland, Texas. With a rod in hand before he could ride a bike, he learned every creek channel, lily pad line, and sunrise sweet spot across Lake Livingston, Sam Rayburn, and the hidden honey holes in between.

After years of tournaments and weekend trips with friends, Luke realized what he loved most was teaching the craft—watching someone feel the first thump on the line, seeing kids light up when a largemouth breaks the surface, and helping families reconnect outdoors. That spirit launched Luke’s Guide Services.

Today, Luke combines hometown hospitality with dialed-in local expertise. He supplies the clean, comfortable boat, top-notch tackle, and patient coaching so you can relax and reel in the fun. From family values to trophy-chasing techniques, Luke is committed to sharing the joy of Southeast Texas bass fishing with every guest.
